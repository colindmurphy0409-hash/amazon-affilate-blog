Body grooming marketing gets loud fast. Manscaped turned groin trimming into a lifestyle brand; dozens of copycats followed. The MERIDIAN Trimmer Original sits in the middle — no viral unboxing, but a large share of Amazon's body-grooming sales and a reputation for doing the basics well.

We looked at what you actually get for the money.

## What you are buying

The MERIDIAN is a cordless, waterproof trimmer built for body hair — chest, legs, underarms, bikini line, and yes, below the belt. It ships with:

- The trimmer itself (ceramic blade system)
- Two adjustable guide combs
- USB charging cable
- Cleaning brush

There is no LED spotlight, no travel case, no second shaver head. MERIDIAN kept the box simple.

## Who this is for

This trimmer fits a few clear use cases:

- **First-time body groomers** who want something safe and straightforward
- **Shower groomers** — IPX7 waterproof rating means you can trim wet
- **Budget-conscious buyers** who do not want to pay Manscaped prices for the same job
- **Women and men** — MERIDIAN markets it unisex, and the slim head works for bikini lines and underarms too

Skip it if you need skin-close shaving. This is a **trimmer**, not a foil shaver. You will get neat, short hair — not a razor-smooth finish.

## How it performs

**Sensitive areas:** The ceramic blade and rounded design are the main selling point. Most users report fewer nicks than a standard multi-groomer on groin and bikini areas. That said, technique still matters — pull skin taut, go slow, use a guard until you know what length you want.

**Larger areas:** The trimmer head is relatively narrow. Chest and leg grooming takes a few extra minutes compared to a wide-blade body groomer. For occasional touch-ups, that is fine. If you are clearing a full back or heavy chest hair weekly, a wider tool might save time.

**Battery and charging:** USB charging is convenient. Plan on roughly 90 minutes to full charge. Runtime is enough for a full-body session; just charge it between uses rather than mid-trim.

**Waterproof claim:** Shower use works as advertised. Rinse the head under the tap after — hair clogs build up fast if you skip cleaning.

## What to watch for

**Replacement blades:** Like any trimmer, the ceramic blade dulls over months of use. MERIDIAN sells replacements — factor that into long-term cost.

**Comb learning curve:** The adjustable guards take a minute to figure out. Start at a longer setting and work down.

**Not medical-grade smooth:** If your goal is completely bare skin, add a razor or a dedicated foil shaver for finishing passes.

## Bottom line

The MERIDIAN Trimmer Original earns its spot on best-seller lists for a reason: it handles the awkward grooming jobs most people actually need, it survives the shower, and it does not require a subscription or a branded toiletry bag.

For everyday body hair maintenance at a fair price, it is an easy recommendation.
